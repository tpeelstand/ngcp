About Me

Natasha G. is making her mark in the Honolulu, HI area’s photography industry. She is the epitome of a free-thinker and a go-getter who truly believes in Joie De Vivre”, French for the “Joy of Life.” She enjoys outdoor adventure such as skydiving, rock climbing, cliff jumping and she’s when she’s afraid to take risks she does it anyway! As a wife, mother, daughter, sister and friend quality time with the people she loves is important to her. She works and lives with both passion and purpose, seeking to empower women through beautiful lady portraits, glamour photography, and boudoir noir photography. She strives to capture the spirit and strength of every client while expressing their unique personalities and pushing the limits. Her motto is “Trust your confidence and calm who you can with your confidence.” She understands everyone isn’t receptive to her views and she is more than ok with that because...confidence!

"When words become unclear, I shall focus with photographs. When images become inadequate, I shall be content with silence."

Ansel Adams
